# LinkedIn Post Recipe

## Section 1: Hook
- The first line must grab attention.
- Use a bold statement, a surprising statistic, or a contrarian viewpoint.
- Keep it under 50 characters.

## Section 2: The Story / Core Message
- Break down the main topic into digestible points.
- Use white space; avoid long paragraphs.
- Keep sentences short and punchy.
- Share personal insights or a brief anecdote if applicable.

## Section 3: The "How-To" or Actionable Advice
- Provide 1-3 practical takeaways.
- Use bullet points or numbered lists.
- Make it something the reader can apply immediately.

## Section 4: Call to Action (CTA)
- Ask a specific question to encourage comments.
- Invite readers to share their thoughts or experiences.
- Keep it friendly and open-ended.
