# Color Psychology

## Color-Emotion Mapping

### Red
- **Hex**: #E53935
- **Associated Emotions**: urgency, anger, passion, excitement, fear, fomo
- **Psychological Effect**: Creates a sense of immediate action and heightened alertness. Raises heart rate and stimulates decisiveness.
- **Use When**: Content demands immediate attention, drives urgency, or signals a critical turning point.

### Blue
- **Hex**: #1E88E5
- **Associated Emotions**: trust, calm, clarity, hope, empathy, inspiration
- **Psychological Effect**: Conveys reliability and professionalism. Reduces anxiety and promotes a sense of order and credibility.
- **Use When**: Content builds credibility, shares research or insights, or fosters trust-based relationships.

### Green
- **Hex**: #43A047
- **Associated Emotions**: optimism, growth, hope, calm, empathy
- **Psychological Effect**: Signals progress, renewal, and balance. Associated with positive outcomes and sustainable success.
- **Use When**: Content celebrates milestones, advocates for growth mindsets, or promotes wellbeing.

### Yellow
- **Hex**: #FDD835
- **Associated Emotions**: curiosity, optimism, surprise, inspiration, awe
- **Psychological Effect**: Captures attention and stimulates mental activity. Triggers enthusiasm and a forward-looking mindset.
- **Use When**: Content opens with a question, challenges conventional thinking, or sparks fresh perspective.

### Purple
- **Hex**: #8E24AA
- **Associated Emotions**: awe, inspiration, curiosity, pride, mystery
- **Psychological Effect**: Evokes sophistication, depth, and creative thinking. Suggests rarity and elevated perspective.
- **Use When**: Content is visionary, explores unconventional ideas, or aims to inspire deep reflection.

### Orange
- **Hex**: #FB8C00
- **Associated Emotions**: enthusiasm, optimism, inspiration, fomo, surprise
- **Psychological Effect**: Combines the urgency of red with the positivity of yellow. Drives engagement and social energy.
- **Use When**: Content promotes community, encourages participation, or creates excitement around opportunity.

### Black
- **Hex**: #212121
- **Associated Emotions**: authority, pride, sophistication, frustration, seriousness
- **Psychological Effect**: Commands attention with minimal distraction. Projects authority, weight, and finality.
- **Use When**: Content carries a strong authoritative stance, delivers hard truths, or emphasizes high-stakes outcomes.

### White
- **Hex**: #FAFAFA
- **Associated Emotions**: clarity, calm, neutrality, simplicity
- **Psychological Effect**: Provides visual breathing room and clarity. Removes noise so the message takes center stage.
- **Use When**: Content must cut through complexity and deliver a single, undiluted idea.
